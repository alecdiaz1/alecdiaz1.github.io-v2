UCI Anthology 2018-2019

Currently leading the design team for the UCI Anthology 2018-2019

Summary

I am currently serving as the design editor for the 2018-2019 UCI Anthology. As design editor, I coordinate the design and layout of the yearbook. So far, I have created a few templates and a style guide for other yearbook editors to follow. I will also be responsible for leading a design team made up of five interns; this involves teaching Adobe software, reviewing and critiquing designs, and meeting deadlines. This is an ongoing project that I will be updating throughout the year!
